WarBoats

WarBoats is a final project I made for my android development class. It's a one sided battleship-like game that is heavily influenced by by a mini game in The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker. The game will randomly place 3 boats, one that is 2 squares in size, one that is 3 squares in size, and one that is 4 squares in size. You have a lkimited number of shots to destroy all the boats in and the game will keep your best score. It's a simple and yet fun time waster. I haven't made any changes to it since I finished the class, yet it's still installed on my phone and I still use it just to kill time every now and again.
